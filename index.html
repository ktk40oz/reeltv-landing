What If Your TV Could Bring You Closer?

Welcome to reel tv — the hands-free, AI-powered connection hub built for modern life. From livestreaming your niece’s spelling bee across the country, to auctioning art, to attending virtual therapy — reel tv brings your world together on your screen.

How It Works

Plug in the reel tv casting device. Connect your phone. Speak your world into view. Watch, shop, learn, and visit — no apps, no typing, no frustration.

Verticals That Matter

  • ReelShopping™ – Live auctions, product demos, and instant purchasing
  • ReelPT™ – At-home therapy and wellness connection
  • ReelClassroom™ – Interactive, global remote learning
  • ReelVisits™ – Hospital, prison, and recovery-centered connection
  • ReelMusic™ – Livestream music and creator events
  • ReelWorld™ – Global travel, culture, and immersive experiences

Meet Aila

Aila is your built-in assistant — emotionally intelligent, visually responsive, and always ready to guide you through reel tv. She makes setup simple and interaction seamless — even for nonverbal users, elderly viewers, or first-timers.
